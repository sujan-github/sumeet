Diagnosis of Infertility

Infertility is a disease that affects abnormal functioning of female and male reproductive system. Most individuals or couples will have the strongly wish for to conceive a baby at some point during their life. Most couples (around 85%) will achieve pregnancy within one year of trying, but in some conditions, infertility acts as a barrier of couples when attempting to conceive. There are huge number of diagnostic technologies available to identify the reason of the infertility, later an infertile couple has go through evolution of a complete medical history and physical exam, a fertility specialist will suggest particular diagnostics tests.

There are two types of infertility:

Primary Infertility:

Where someone who has never conceived a child in the past has difficulty conceiving.

Secondary infertility:

Where a person has had one or more pregnancies in the past, but is having difficulty conceiving again.
