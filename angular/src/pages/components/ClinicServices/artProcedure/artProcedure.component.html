In Vitro Fertilization:

In Vitro Fertilization is beneficial for women who are infertile due to blocked Fallopian tubes, Anovulation,Decreased ovarian Reserve,Anti Sperm Antibodies,Poly Cystic Ovaries and unexplained infertiity to have their own genetic baby. Several infertility problems can be determined & overcome through this IVF process.

ICSI:

ICSI gives you and your partner an opportunity to have a baby and it is the main treatment for those who are having male infertility issues such as low sperm count,low sperm motility,increase in abnormal sperm morphology and Azoospermia.

Assisted Hatching:

This technique commonly is used during the IVF procedure to improve success rates and it is suitable for patients more than 35 years of age as the outer shell/Zona of the embryo becomes thickened and might prevent hatching and thereby implantation.

Intra Uterine Insemination:

This is one type of assisted reproductive procedure in wished the semen sample is washed and the good quality sperm is isolated and then injected into the uterus of the female partner thereb enabling fertilisation and pregnancy.

Embryo Transfer:

This is the major part in your IVF treatment; by using a Embryo transfer catheter we can transfer embryos into the uterus of the female to create pregnancy.
